About Us

Marilyn was honored to receive a Top 100 QuickBooks ProAdvisor award for 2016.

Nimbus Consulting was founded by Marilyn Sudbeck, CPA to represent the future of small- to mid-size business computing.  The future of computing is “in the clouds.”  Nimbus is the rain cloud; Nimbus Consulting will be working with all of you who are rain makers.  However, her background is on desktop computing and, of course, she still remains a strong consultant in desktop, laptop and server environments.

Marilyn began working with QuickBooks in 1995 when she recommended QuickBooks to a community college for small business software classes.  She was one of the first QuickBooks ProAdvisors, long before Intuit even offered certification.  In 2003, Marilyn left the corporate world and began consulting with QuickBooks full time.

She works with all versions of QuickBooks, all years of QuickBooks and many 3rd party add-ons.  She has taught classes at Colorado Free University, Red Rocks Community College, CPE classes at the University of Denver Graduate Tax Program, and a computer learning center.  She has been a speaker at the Illinois Society of CPAs Business and Technology Solutions Conference, the Colorado Society of CPAs, the Sleeter Group National Conference for QuickBooks ProAdvisors and the Rocky Mountain Area Conference for Finance and Accounting Professionals.   She currently teaches QuickBooks seminars for CPAs, EAs and bookkeepers at CPE4U Colorado.  www.CPE4UColorado.com

She served on Intuit’s national advisory council for Intuit Solution Providers, now called Intuit Premier Resellers.  The Intuit Premier Resellers bring high level expertise to QuickBooks Pro & Premier, QuickBooks Enterprise, and QuickBooks Point of Sale. Marilyn has worked with Intuit in developing training materials for consultants who want to become Certified ProAdvisors in Point of Sale. 

Marilyn is the only Colorado member of Intuit’s Accounting Professionals Trainer/Writer Network.  This network has a total of about 150 members nationwide.
